About

Bio

Shubham is a software developer based in the Lucknow, India. He delights in simple, elegant solutions to problems and enjoys writing modular, documented, well-tested code. After graduating from college with a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree, Shubham's lifelong love for tech drove him to pursue a career in programming. He is a firm believer in the value of open-source software and can't imagine life without Vim.

Outside of software, Shubham enjoys spending time with his friends, movies, high fantasy novels, learning new things, playing cricket, studying Indian culture, listening to Eminem, DJ Khaled, Rihanna, Drake and Linkin Park, and generally creating order out of chaos. And making lists.
